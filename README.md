## About Me

> **“Daily effort, step-by-step persistence, and proactive learning are the most sustainable paths to long-term growth.”**

My name is **Pham Nguyen Minh Chau** (*mmchouuu*), an **Information Technology student at Saigon University**.  
I am aiming to become a **Software Engineer**, with a strong foundation in **Business Analysis (BA)** and **Web Application Development**.

I am particularly interested in:
- Requirements analysis, business process modeling, and system workflows (BA)
- Designing and developing websites using **fullstack microservice architectures**
- Building systems that are clear, maintainable, and practically applicable

To me, programming is not just about writing code, but about **transforming business problems into effective and sustainable software solutions**.

---

## What I Do

I have hands-on experience across multiple roles in the software development lifecycle:

- **Business Analysis (BA):** requirements analysis, business workflows, system modeling  
- **Frontend Development:** building user-friendly and scalable web interfaces  
- **Backend Development:** API design, business logic implementation, stability and security  
- **Database Design:** schema design, querying, optimization, and data integrity  

My goal is to deliver systems that are **well-structured, maintainable, and highly practical**.
